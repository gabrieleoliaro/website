Gabriele Oliaro

Milan, Italy · Cambridge, MA · gabriele_oliaro@college.harvard.edu

Gabriele is a Junior at Harvard College studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. His main academic interests are Signal Processing and Computer Systems. He has research experience in the fields of Machine Learning and Computer Systems, and he is currently working in Prof. Eddie Kohler's lab.

Before college, Gabriele lived in Milan, Italy, where he graduated from Liceo Classico (Classical studies high school) Istituto Leone XIII. He competed in the Italian Olympiads in Informatics at the regional level, and was involved in swimming, running, and Ancient Greek Theater. He spent his Junior year at Vashon Island High School (Vashon, WA), where he was part of the Varsity XC Team and was Captain of the Varsity T&F team.

Gabriele is fluent in Italian and English, and he is proficient in Ancient Greek and Latin. In addition to engineering, Gabriele is enthusiastic about reading, outdoor activities and cooking.
